# Initial Concept
This project is an advanced VEX V5 autonomous robotics template focused on precise, algorithm-driven motion planning and control for the 2025-2026 season of Push-Back.

# Product Definition

## Goals
- Improve autonomous routine precision and reliability.
- Implement advanced motion algorithms like Boomerang and Odometry.

## Target Users
- VEX V5 Competition teams looking for advanced autonomous routines.
- High-performance teams requiring high-speed and accurate pathing.

## Key Features
- Integrated IMU and Odometry for field navigation.
- Support for distance resets using distance sensors.
- Boomerang and CurveCircle motion algorithms.

## Success Metrics
- Accuracy of autonomous movements (e.g., error margin < 1 inch).
- Speed of execution for routines during competition.

## Field Goals
- Specifically designed for the 2025-2026 season of Push-Back.
